The Family Business.

Established over 25 years ago, by Derek Hertzog. Our yard is situated down viaduct road and we have been operating here since the 90s. We started life as a scrap metal yard, which some of the locals might remember as ‘Manor Motors’. We purchased our first skip truck in the late 90’s, delivering mini skips to the Altrincham area.

From there we closed down our scrap metal yard in 2004, allowing us the space we needed to continue to grow as a skip hire company, since then we have grown to offer mini, midi and maxi skips. Together Derek and his son Ian have worked hard to provide Altrincham and the surrounding areas with a reliable skip hire service.

After the passing of our beloved Derek Hertzog, Ian’s wife and daughter joined the business. Helping to support the growth and help to continue to deliver the high-quality service that is expected.

Altrincham Skip Hire – It’s the service that counts.
